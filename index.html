    Our Humble Beginnings

    BeakerData is an online science platform for kids k-12 to log and track their own projects inside and outside the classroom. Global collaboration will help build our future. Allowing children to store the details, photos, and videos of their science experiments; as well as publish papers on their projects. This will allow kids to pave their own way to collaboration opportunities with peers, data collection for future scholarships, and mentors from the scientific community.
